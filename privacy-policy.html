Electro Wallet

Non-custodial Bitcoin Wallet — Privacy Policy

Privacy First

Your Keys. Your Bitcoin. Your Privacy.

Electro Wallet is a self-custody, open-source Bitcoin wallet. We built it with a single principle: we should never be able to access your funds or your data — because we never touch them in the first place.

Last updated: June 27, 2026  •  Effective date: June 27, 2026

1. Overview

🔒 Non-Custodial by Design

Electro Wallet is a non-custodial Bitcoin wallet. This means:

  • Your seed phrase (recovery phrase) is generated entirely on your device and stored exclusively in your device's Keychain. We never see it, transmit it, or have any way to recover it.
  • Your private keys never leave your device in any form.
  • We cannot move, freeze, or access your funds. Only you hold the keys.
If you lose your seed phrase and your device, no one — including us — can recover your funds. Please back it up securely offline.
2. Data We Collect

🚫 We Collect No Personal Data

Electro Wallet does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your IP address or device identifiers
  • Your Bitcoin addresses or transaction history
  • Your seed phrase, private keys, or PIN
  • Crash reports or analytics
  • Location data
  • Any usage telemetry or behavioral data

There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, and no servers operated by Electro Wallet that receive your data.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

📱 What Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us:

  • Seed phrase — encrypted in iOS Keychain (kSecClassGenericPassword), accessible only by Electro Wallet on your device.
  • PIN — stored in iOS Keychain; used for local app lock only.
  • App preferences — PIN/biometric enabled flags stored in UserDefaults; no sensitive financial data.
  • Wallet database — a local SQLite file (via BitcoinKit.Swift) caching block headers and transactions for SPV validation. This file is not transmitted anywhere.

All of this data is removed if you choose "Delete Wallet" in Settings or uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

🌐 Bitcoin Network Connectivity

To sync your wallet with the Bitcoin blockchain, Electro Wallet connects to public Bitcoin infrastructure. These connections may reveal your IP address to those services:

  • BitcoinKit.Swift (open source library) — used for all Bitcoin protocol operations. It connects to public Bitcoin full nodes and the following public APIs for transaction history:
    • blocksdecoded.com — transaction indexing API
    • blockchain.info — fallback API for address data
  • blockchain.info / CoinGecko (price feeds) — used to display the current BTC/USD exchange rate. Only a public market price endpoint is queried; no wallet data is sent.
Your Bitcoin addresses may be visible to these public APIs as part of normal SPV (Simplified Payment Verification) operation. This is inherent to how lightweight Bitcoin clients work. For maximum privacy, consider running your own Bitcoin node.

👀 Apple Services

As an iOS app, Electro Wallet runs on Apple's platform and is subject to Apple's standard privacy practices:

  • Face ID / Touch ID — biometric authentication is handled entirely by iOS (LocalAuthentication framework). Biometric data never leaves your device and is never accessible to Electro Wallet or any third party.
  • iCloud Keychain — Electro Wallet does not enable iCloud sync for Keychain items. Your seed phrase is stored with kSecAttrSynchronizable = false, meaning it remains on-device only.
  • App Store — Apple may collect analytics related to app downloads and crashes through the App Store, per Apple's Privacy Policy.
5. Security

🛡 Security Measures

  • iOS Keychain — your seed phrase and PIN are stored with hardware-backed encryption.
  • App Lock — optional PIN and biometric lock prevents unauthorized access if your device is unlocked.
  • No network transmission of secrets — your seed phrase and private keys are never sent over any network connection.
  • Open source — the app's source code is publicly auditable so anyone can verify these claims.
Security of your funds ultimately depends on keeping your seed phrase secret and your device secure. Enable a device passcode and app PIN for best protection.
6. Children's Privacy

👶 Not Directed at Children

Electro Wallet is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we collect no personal data from anyone, this applies equally to all users.

7. Changes to This Policy

📝 Policy Updates

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users through the app or the App Store release notes.

Because we collect no personal data, changes are unlikely to affect how your information is handled — but we are committed to keeping this policy accurate and transparent.

8. Open Source

📚 Verify Our Claims

Electro Wallet is open source. You can inspect the full source code to verify that the app behaves exactly as described in this policy. We encourage security researchers and privacy advocates to audit the code.

The app is powered by BitcoinKit.Swift, also an open-source library.
